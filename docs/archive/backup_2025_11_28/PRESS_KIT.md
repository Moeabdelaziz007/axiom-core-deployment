## Company Background

### Founding Story

Axiom was founded in 2024 by a team of AI researchers, blockchain experts, and cultural specialists who recognized a critical gap in the AI landscape: the complete absence of truly bilingual AI platforms that understand Arabic culture and business practices.

The founding team, based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, experienced firsthand the challenges of using Western AI platforms that only translated Arabic rather than understanding it. This led to a mission to create an AI platform that would truly serve the Arabic-speaking world while being ready for the future of Web3 technology.
